# Youtube-trending-analysis-2026

BUSINESS PROBLEM
YouTube's platform hosts millions of videos, but only a small percentage become trending content. The company wants to understand the factors that drive video popularity and audience engagement. By analyzing views, likes, comments, categories, publishing times, and channel performance, YouTube can identify patterns associated with successful videos. These insights can help creators optimize their content strategies and improve audience reach. The goal is to use data-driven recommendations to increase engagement and maximize the likelihood of videos appearing on the trending page.
